The AndyOlympics

YOU'VE heard of the Olympics and the Paralympics, but have you heard of the AndyOlympics? Well you have now. It's the brainchild of Kirribilli's Andrew Hannan, who will spend the next 12 months attempting what is believed to be a world first - competing in 130 events across every male Olympic sport.
